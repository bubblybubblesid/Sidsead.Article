Street sweeper dead, one injured after post raid in Antipolo

Published May 14,2024

ANTIPOLO, Rizal — This weekend, a power plant collapsed in Antipolo City, Rizal's Barangay Dela Paz, killing a street sweeper and injuring another.

Following the collision on Saturday, the 57-year-old street sweeper—who was riding a contemporary tricycle—died, according to PLtCol. Police chief of Antipolo City, Ryan Manongdo.

The woman who fell on a post and onto the trike while riding it is shown in the video.

This also affected residents who were without energy for one or two weeks due to a power plant accident.
